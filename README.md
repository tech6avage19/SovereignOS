# SovereignOS — The Human-First Digital Sovereignty Stack

SovereignOS is a decentralized, modular, and privacy-first computing platform that puts **you** in control of your data, identity, AI, and digital life.

---

## What SovereignOS Offers

- 🏠 **Home Nodes**: Your personal, secure root environment  
- 📱 **Mobile Nodes**: Portable edge devices that follow you  
- 🌐 **Satellite Nodes**: Encrypted sync points that protect privacy  
- 🔎 **ZeeT Search**: Offline, decentralized, and anonymous search engine  
- 🔐 **Sanctum**: Zero-knowledge encrypted backup vault  
- 🤖 **AI Agents**: Local-first, task-scoped helpers under your control  
- ⚡ **Crypto & Identity**: Built-in Bitcoin, Lightning, and decentralized IDs  

---

## Philosophy

SovereignOS is about digital independence by design.  
You own your data. You own your identity. You own your AI.

It’s an ecosystem built to resist surveillance, censorship, and centralized control — empowering people to be sovereign in a connected world.
